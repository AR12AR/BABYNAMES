Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2025. Your privacy matters to us.

1. Information We Collect

BABYNAMES does not require registration or account creation. We collect minimal information necessary to provide our services.

  • Search queries (names and letters) — used only to generate results
  • Basic analytics data (page views, country, browser type) via Google Analytics
  • Favourites saved locally in your browser (we do not have access to this data)

2. How We Use Information

We use the information collected to:

  • Improve our name database and search results
  • Understand how users interact with the website
  • Display relevant advertisements through Google AdSense

3. Cookies

We use cookies for analytics and advertising purposes. By using BABYNAMES, you consent to our use of cookies. You can disable cookies in your browser settings at any time.

4. Third-Party Advertising

We use Google AdSense to display advertisements. Google may use cookies to serve ads based on your prior visits to our website or other websites. You can opt out of personalized advertising by visiting Google's Ads Settings.

5. Data Security

We take reasonable precautions to protect your information. All data transmission is encrypted via HTTPS. Your favourites are stored only in your local browser and are never transmitted to our servers.

6. Children's Privacy

BABYNAMES is designed for expectant parents and does not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

7. Contact

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us through our Contact page.
